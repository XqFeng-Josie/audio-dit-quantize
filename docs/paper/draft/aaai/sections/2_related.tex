\section{Related Work}
\paragraph{PTQ for LLMs and calibration data.}
SmoothQuant migrates activation outliers into weights \citep{xiao2023smoothquant}; GPTQ and AWQ reconstruct weights layer-wise \citep{frantar2023gptq,lin2024awq}; QuaRot, SpinQuant, and FlatQuant remove outliers with Hadamard, learned, and continuously optimized affine rotations, respectively \citep{ashkboos2024quarot,liu2025spinquant,sun2025flatquant}. The role of calibration data in this pipeline is comparatively understudied: \citet{williams2024impact} find LLM PTQ broadly insensitive to calibration-set choice, and concurrent work optimizes calibration mixtures for task-aware LLM quantization \citep{wang2026tasa}. Calibration \emph{selection} is studied outside the LLM literature too: SelectQ clusters per-layer activation statistics for CNN classifiers \citep{zhang2025selectq}, and concurrent work learns per-sample weights that align cross-timestep gradients for image-diffusion PTQ \citep{hoang2026gradcalib}, the closest prior work to our data-side study in spirit. Both optimize a statistical or gradient criterion with no task structure, and neither has been tested outside vision; we contribute controlled comparisons and dose-response curves over task-defined composition instead, plus a preregistered cross-scale replication.
 
\paragraph{PTQ for visual diffusion and DiTs.}
Q-Diffusion and PTQ4DM identified timestep-varying activation statistics as the core difficulty \citep{li2023qdiffusion,shang2023ptq4dm}; TDQ learns per-timestep quantization parameters \citep{so2023tdq}; ViDiT-Q and MixDQ adapt PTQ to DiT backbones for image and video, where protecting \emph{early} (high-noise) denoising steps and content-bearing cross-attention is the received wisdom \citep{zhao2025viditq,zhao2024mixdq}; SVDQuant absorbs outliers into a low-rank branch at W4A4 \citep{li2025svdquant}. ViDiT-Q's metric-decoupled precision allocation is the closest prior mixed-precision study to ours; we differ in domain (speech, not image quality), in mechanism depth (an error taxonomy tracing module sensitivity to a specific error class, repetition-collapse deletions), and, most directly, in evidence: measured TTS sensitivity points the opposite way on both axes, a reversal unreadable from the vision result.
 
\paragraph{Efficient speech synthesis.}
Speech-side quantization has targeted vocoders and on-device pipelines with 8-bit integer arithmetic \citep{valin2022shoestring,vipperla2020bunched}, and recently extreme low-bit TTS via quantization-aware retraining \citep{kawamura2025bittts}. Two contemporaneous PTQ papers come closest to ours: \citet{khandelwal2025ptqaudio} and \citet{vora2025ptq4adm} quantize text-to-audio diffusion models, general sound generation rather than speech, to W8A8/W4A8, both reporting sharp W4A4 degradation; a short single-author paper quantizes an autoregressive, LLaMA-based voice-cloning TTS system with limited methodological detail \citep{zain2026lightweight}. None targets a diffusion-based TTS backbone or reports intelligibility and speaker similarity jointly. Step-count reduction by distillation \citep{yin2024dmd,yin2024dmd2,li2025dmospeech} is orthogonal to bit-width reduction and composable with it.
